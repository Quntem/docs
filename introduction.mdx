## What do we do?

We are an <Tooltip tip="a small group of developers">indie development team</Tooltip> that makes <Tooltip tip="Quntem Creator, Quntem Cloud+ & Voxa">closed source</Tooltip> and <Tooltip tip="Clatter and Cards of Massacre">open sourced</Tooltip> tools to help anyone. This includes Quntem Creator (for graphic designers), Cloud+ (for companies), Clatter (for companies) and Voxa (for casual users).

<Note>Cloud+ and Voxa are not available to the public yet.</Note>

## What is Quntem?

Quntem is the name of our company. All our tools and services proudly carry the "Quntem" name as a prefix.
For example, instead of saying "Creator," you should say "Quntem Creator." The Quntem name represents our commitment to quality and innovation. Please use it respectfully and accurately.

<Info>We are an unregistered company.</Info>
